The App displays banner advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymized data such as device type, usage patterns, and general location (e.g. country or city) to serve relevant ads.

2. Data Collection

This App does not collect, store, or transmit any user data, including names, email addresses, or contacts.

This App is designed for general audiences. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will remove the data immediately.
